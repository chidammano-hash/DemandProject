# Interview Call Playbook - Vice President, Data Science & AI

## 1) Resume upgrades that materially increase VP interview calls
- Show org scale clearly: direct reports, total team size, managers reporting to you, hiring velocity.
- Show business ownership: budget, ROI, P&L influence, and enterprise KPI accountability.
- Show production AI outcomes: adoption, cycle-time reduction, hours saved, revenue/profit/risk impact.
- Show governance maturity: model risk controls, responsible AI practices, compliance and audit readiness.
- Show executive influence: board/executive presentations, enterprise steering committees, cross-BU alignment.

## 2) Positioning statement to use in resume + LinkedIn
- "VP-level AI and Data Science leader driving enterprise decision intelligence, agentic AI workflows, and measurable business outcomes at scale."

## 3) Story architecture for VP interviews
- Strategy: How you prioritize AI portfolio and sequence value.
- Operating model: How you structure DS, MLE, data engineering, product, and MLOps collaboration.
- Delivery: How models move from experimentation to production with reliability.
- Adoption: How you drive business behavior change and trust in AI outputs.
- Governance: How you reduce risk while increasing speed.

## 4) 30-60-90 day plan (prepare before interviews)
- 30 days: assess team, platform maturity, AI use-case portfolio, and governance gaps.
- 60 days: launch value-focused AI roadmap and operating cadence with business leadership.
- 90 days: deliver 2-3 quick-win AI products and establish executive KPI dashboard.

## 5) Application strategy
- Prioritize referral-led applications for VP/Head roles.
- Apply to fewer roles with deeper tailoring (15-25 highly matched roles).
- Use role-specific headline in resume file name and top summary.
- Send recruiter note with 3 metrics: team scale, budget owned, one flagship AI outcome.

## 6) Immediate actions (next 7 days)
- Finalize this VP resume with missing quant metrics in AI/agentic section.
- Update LinkedIn headline/About to match VP AI positioning.
- Build a one-page AI transformation portfolio with 3 case studies.
- Prepare 8 leadership stories: org design, executive alignment, failure recovery, and governance trade-offs.
